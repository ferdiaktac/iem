Who we are

Integrated Energy Materials is an American corporation whose mission is to build a world class battery supply chain, from battery metals to battery cells. We enable the innovation and social investments necessary to support the sustainable decarbonization of the global economy.

Batteries will power the green economy—from electric vehicles to distributed energy storage, smartphones to defense applications.

IEM integrates mined battery metals with refined and processed materials with battery manufacturing. This approach serves to advance quality, transparency, and strengthened supply certainty. Driving technological innovation at a moment critical to the creation of the new green economy.
