Built on a base comprised of world-class battery metal mining assets, Integrated Energy Materials is focused on developing innovative large-scale refining, cathode and battery cell manufacturing required to drive decarbonization.

These pan-continental operations will be fully integrated to ensure the quality, transparency, and security of supply required to ensure the sustainability of the transition to the green economy.

Our leadership team has decades of experience driving change and building institutions from Government to the Military to Fortune 500 companies. Across North America, Africa, the Middle East, South Asia, and Europe.
